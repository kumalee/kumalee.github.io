    EditorConfig helps developers define and maintain consistent coding styles between different editors and IDEs. The EditorConfig project consists of a file format for defining coding styles and a collection of text editor plugins that enable editors to read the file format and adhere to defined styles. Gossip Renaissance

I bought 《重返文艺复兴－你不知道的天才之争》 two month ago.

I’m really interesting at the European Renaissance of the 14th–17th centuries. There were many great people, such as Michelangelo and Leonardo da Vinci. They were the “Renaissance man”. People are always pay more attention to gossip than normal news. I want to rename the book 《重返文艺复兴－天才的撕逼大战》.
It’s more funny, is it?

I think the most interesting things are the two famous battles:

  1. Lorenzo Ghiberti VS Filippo Brunelleschi
    One proposed starting point is 1401, when the rival geniuses Lorenzo Ghiberti and Filippo Brunelleschi competed for the contract to build the bronze doors for the Baptistery of the Florence Cathedral (Ghiberti won)
  2. Michelangelo(Battle of Càscina) VS Leonardo da Vinci(Battle of Anghiari)

Tips:

  1. 我们经常把 Leonardo da Vinci 的那幅传世名作叫做《蒙娜丽莎》，但这幅画在法国被称为《乔宫多夫人》，
    在意大利被称作《乔康达夫人》。画中的女子本名：丽莎·盖拉尔迪尼，是佛罗伦萨富商乔宫多（后宫比较多嘛？(⊙ω⊙)）的妻子。
    蒙娜丽莎(Mona Lisa) 意思是丽莎夫人，但是 Monna 才是 Modonna (夫人)的正确缩写。
  2. Leonardo da Vinci 在《Monna Lisa》上运用了独创的晕涂法(Sfumato)
